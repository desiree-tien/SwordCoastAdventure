KELLENT SUITHRASAS ELF FIGHTER Kellen was trained from childhood by a gruff but warmhearted former soldier to battle Goblang, the gang of goblins that ruled the region. One summer eve, the Goblang King sent a pair of his finest warriors to slaughter the people of Kellen's village, but Kellen towered over the small creatures and treated them to a humiliating beating. The Goblang King was thoroughly embarassed and enraged and ambushed Kellen's master in the woods. He then sent his entire horde to defeat Kellen and his village. While the master healed, Kellen trained a newly confident band of warrior farmers. The Goblang goblins were no match for the fair-haired blademaster and his agricultural army.
