Captain's Log

Session 1: January 28, 2018

First Day In Town


Upon arriving in the marketplace the adventurers eview the notice board(After Kellin steals a small child, and Cher has to chase down its mother to return it safely) and see 3 various requests for help:

  • Requesting Test Subjects. Rewards: 100 G a Vial
  • Find a missing cow. Rewards: Sack of gold
  • Tame an Owlbear by end of week for a Show. Rewards: 6 Tickets to the show and 1% of net ticket sales

Arken wanders off as the group debates and begins to follow the Duke and his guards. After presenting herself to the Duke he suggests a dual between Arken and the leader of the guards, Morwin. (Who happens to be his daughter).
After being victorious against Morwin, the Duke requests assistance in locating 4 misisng cards for the town wizard Delphin. He offers up a cart and Osmar (a local solider, and father of the child Kellin stole previously). Reward: 25 gold pieces per person, and 1 week room and board at the local Inn
Rufus not inclinded to help, but Arken has promised her portion to him for his assitance.

The Group Departs

While the trip is smooth sailing at first, the group is attacked by a Bug Bear and two Goblins. Working together they are able to defeat the Bugbear, and one of the goblins, the other one runs away. The fight was not without it's struggles. Osmar took an arrow to the shoulder, and Gher was shot in the thigh.
Post fight, the wounds were cleaned and cauterized. The group rested during the night with Arken and Kellin taking various watches throughout the night.
Rufus, who is developing a relationship with his horse Mary has been collecting snozberries for her. Mary likes snozberries.


Session 0: January 14, 2018

We rolled our five daring adventurers: Erg, Kellen, River, Rufus, and Arken. For more information about each adventurer, visit the Adventurers page.


PCs found a way for each character to have a loose association with the others. Erg, Kellent, and Arken arrived in the port of Daggerford on a ship in distress. River, the Water Genasi with the unique ability to breathe water, towed their small ship into port.


We learned about Daggerford, it's ruling hierarchy (The Duke), and the surrounding areas. We learned about the larger Sword Coast and the larger regions surrounding the coastline. This includes numerous forests, mountain ranges, and even a big ol' desert.
